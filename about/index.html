About

Hello, I’m Nikhil Pawar.

I hold both a Bachelor’s and Master’s degree in Information Systems from Pace University, New York. My professional focus lies in transforming complex datasets into structured, decision-ready intelligence that supports measurable business performance.

I combine analytical rigor, structured problem-solving, and performance measurement frameworks to help organizations improve reporting visibility, optimize operations, and enable confident executive decision-making.

Analytical Approach

My methodology is built on clarity, precision, and business alignment. Typical engagements include:

  • Data validation and quality assurance to ensure integrity and reliability
  • Exploratory analysis to uncover patterns, trends, and risks
  • KPI framework development aligned with strategic objectives
  • Interactive dashboard architecture for continuous performance tracking
  • Statistical modeling and predictive analysis when required

My objective is not simply to analyze data, but to reduce ambiguity and translate information into actionable business insight.

Technical Expertise

  • Languages: SQL, Python, R
  • Business Intelligence: Power BI, Tableau
  • Data Processing: Pandas, NumPy
  • Modeling: Supervised and Unsupervised Machine Learning
  • Reporting: Executive KPI tracking and performance dashboards

Professional Focus

I am particularly interested in enterprise analytics environments where scalable reporting systems, structured data modeling, and performance optimization directly contribute to measurable business outcomes.

I value documentation, maintainability, and aligning technical implementation with real-world operational needs.
